# Renascimento Técnico

## Product Overview

**The Pitch:** An interactive mobile brand bible for "Nero 27," translating the static concept of a brand board into a fluid, tactile sketchbook experience. It bridges the gap between classical da Vincian aesthetics and modern digital utility.

**For:** Stakeholders, designers, and clients of Nero 27 who value precision, heritage, and the intellectual intersection of art and engineering.

**Device:** Mobile (Vertical scroll, tactile touch interactions)

**Design Direction:** **Da Vinci’s Codex.** A skeuomorphic-adjacent interface that feels like examining a preserved Renaissance manuscript. Texture is paramount—paper grain, ink bleed, construction lines, and golden ratios overlaid on UI elements.

**Inspired by:** *The Codex Atlanticus (Leonardo da Vinci)*, *Paper by WeTransfer*, *Nytimes "T" Magazine typography*.

---

## Screens

- **01 Splash / Cover:** The "Leather Cover" entry point with embossed branding.
- **02 The Emblem (Main Logo):** Detailed analysis of the primary logo with geometric overlays.
- **03 The Mark (Icon/Favicon):** Isolated geometric reduction for small scales.
- **04 The Palette (Colors):** Swatches presented as pigment jars and ink tests.
- **05 The Typeset (Typography):** Specimen sheet resembling early printing press proofs.
- **06 Application (Mockups):** The brand applied to physical artifacts (business card, wax seal).

---

## Key Flows

**Flow: Examining the Logo Geometry**

1. User lands on **02 The Emblem** -> sees the Nero 27 logo sketched on parchment.
2. User toggles "Construction Lines" toggle -> Fine red and blue geometric lines (circles, squares) fade in over the logo to reveal the golden ratio alignment.
3. User taps specific nodes -> Tooltips appear in handwriting font explaining the mathematical derivation.

## Color Palette

- **Primary (Ink):** `#2A231E` - Deep Sepia. Used for primary text and main linework.
- **Background (Parchment):** `#F0EAD6` - Aged Vellum. Subtle grain texture required.
- **Surface (Wash):** `#E6DCC3` - Darker paper sections, active states.
- **Text (Faded Ink):** `#5C4E43` - Secondary text, historical notes.
- **Muted (Graphite):** `#8C8276` - Construction lines, borders.
- **Accent (Sanguine):** `#8A3324` - Red chalk (Sanguine) for highlights, alerts, and focal points.

## Typography

- **Headings:** *Cormorant Garamond*, Italic, 700, 32-40px. (Simulates elegant calligraphy).
- **Body:** *IM Fell English SC*, 400, 16px. (Simulates letterpress imperfection).
- **Small text/Captions:** *Reenie Beanie*, 400, 18px. (Simulates handwritten annotations).
- **Buttons:** *Cormorant Garamond*, SC (Small Caps), 600, 14px.

**Style notes:**
- **Borders:** Double lines (thin-thick-thin) typical of classical drafting.
- **Shadows:** No drop shadows. Use hatching (cross-hatching lines) to denote depth.
- **Texture:** CSS blend mode `multiply` on text to simulate ink soaking into paper.

<summary>Screen Specifications</summary>

### 01 Splash / Cover

**Purpose:** Brand immersion. Sets the tone of opening a physical book.

**Layout:** Centered content. Heavy vertical padding.

**Key Elements:**
- **Background:** Dark brown texture `#1A1510` (Leather binding texture).
- **Embossed Logo:** "Nero 27" centered, gold foil effect (`#C5A059`), inner shadow to look pressed in.
- **Subtitle:** "Brand Codex v.1.0" in *IM Fell English SC*, opacity 60%.
- **"Open" Action:** A decorative wax seal illustration at the bottom.

**Interactions:**
- **Slide Up:** User swipes up (or taps seal) -> Leather background splits or slides up to reveal the Parchment background of Screen 02.

---

### 02 The Emblem (Main Logo)

**Purpose:** Detailed presentation of the primary Vitruvian logo.

**Layout:** Top header (navigation), Main Canvas (Logo), Bottom Controls.

**Key Elements:**
- **Header:** "Fig. 1: The Identity", *Cormorant Garamond Italic*, centered. Sanguine red underline.
- **Main Canvas:**
    - The Logo: "Nero 27" intertwined with a Vitruvian figure and compass circles.
    - Style: Ink bleed edges.
    - Animation: Subtle "sketching" animation on load (SVG `stroke-dashoffset`).
- **Toggle Control:** "Show Geometry" switch. Styled like a mechanical brass slider.
    - **Active:** Overlays fine vector lines (circles, triangles) in Sanguine Red (`#8A3324`) demonstrating the logo's proportions.

**States:**
- **Initial:** Clean logo.
- **Geometry On:** Busy, mathematical overlay active.

---

### 03 The Mark (Icon)

**Purpose:** Showcasing the reductive mark for favicons/apps.

**Layout:** Grid of 3 variations showing scale.

**Key Elements:**
- **Section Title:** "Fig. 2: Reduction & Scale".
- **Large Preview:** 128px square container. The "N27" monogram inside a perfect circle.
- **Medium Preview:** 64px square.
- **Small Preview:** 32px square.
- **Annotation:** Handwritten arrow pointing to the smallest icon with text "Legible at 16px" using *Reenie Beanie* font.

**Components:**
- **Card:** Border: `1px solid #8C8276`. Background: transparent.

---

### 04 The Palette

**Purpose:** Color definition presented as artist materials.

**Layout:** Vertical list of color "swatches".

**Key Elements:**
- **Swatch Item:**
    - Visual: A circle with rough, organic edges (as if painted with a brush).
    - Label: Name of color (e.g., "Deep Sepia") in *Cormorant Garamond*.
    - Value: Hex code in *IM Fell English* (small).
    - CMYK/RGB: Hidden by default, expands on tap.
- **Colors displayed:**
    1. Carbon Black (`#000000`)
    2. Deep Sepia (`#2A231E`)
    3. Sanguine (`#8A3324`)
    4. Aged Vellum (`#F0EAD6`)

**Interactions:**
- **Tap Swatch:** The "paint" circle pulses like liquid. Copied to clipboard notification appears as a handwritten note: "Pigment Copied".

---

### 05 The Typeset

**Purpose:** Typography usage guidelines.

**Layout:** A typesetter's specimen sheet.

**Key Elements:**
- **Drop Cap:** A massive, ornate "N" (Nero) starting a paragraph of Lorem Ipsum.
- **The Alphabet:** A-Z displayed in the primary font (*Cormorant*), taking up full width lines.
- **The Numerals:** 0-9 displayed with mathematical spacing equations between them (e.g., `1 + 2 = 3`).
- **Hierarchy Demo:**
    - H1: "Principia Mathematica" (32px)
    - H2: "The Golden Ratio" (24px)
    - Body: "Proportion is a correspondence among the measures of the members of an entire work..." (16px)

**Components:**
- **Ruler Lines:** Background of this section has faint horizontal blue lines like a notebook.

---

### 06 Application (Footer Variation)

**Purpose:** How the brand lives in constrained spaces (Footer).

**Layout:** Dark mode contrast block.

**Key Elements:**
- **Container:** Full width block, Background `#2A231E` (Deep Sepia).
- **Logo Variant:** The "Horizontal Linear" version of Nero 27 in `#F0EAD6` (Vellum).
- **Context:** Dummy footer links ("Legal", "Contact", "Blueprints") in `opacity-60` white serif.
- **Visual Note:** A "paper note" pinned via CSS to the top right of this dark block saying "Inversion for dark substrates" (Handwritten font).

**Responsive:**
- **Mobile:** Stacked layout.
- **Desktop:** Not applicable (Mobile view specific).

<summary>Build Guide</summary>

**Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3

**Assets:**
1.  **Fonts:** Google Fonts (Cormorant Garamond, IM Fell English SC, Reenie Beanie).
2.  **Textures:** Seamless paper grain PNG (opacity 5%, multiply blend).
3.  **SVGs:** Hand-drawn stroke paths for the logo (exported from Illustrator with preserved strokes for animation).

**Build Order:**
1.  **Screen 04 (Palette):** Define the CSS variables/Tailwind config first. Get the "Paper" feel right (background + texture).
2.  **Screen 05 (Typography):** Establish the type hierarchy.
3.  **Screen 02 (The Emblem):** Implement the complex SVG layering and toggle logic.
4.  **Remaining Screens:** Build out structural content.

**Tailwind Config Nuances:**
- Extend colors with specific names: `bg-parchment`, `text-ink`, `border-sanguine`.
- Add custom font families: `font-serif-display`, `font-hand`.
- Add custom drop-shadow: `box-shadow: 2px 2px 0px #8C8276` (Hard shadow for depth without blur).

</details>

Financial Integrity

Fundamental principles governing the ethical allocation and auditing of capital within the Renascimento ecosystem. Strict adherence is mandatory for all active governance members.

❦

Article 1.1: Transaction Limits

Critical

Any capital transfer exceeding the pre-approved threshold of 50,000 units requires multi-signature authorization from at least two Senior Auditors. Failure to comply results in immediate asset freezing.

Last updated: Oct 12, 2023

Article 1.2: Audit Frequency

Standard

Automated ledger reconciliation occurs every 24 hours. Manual oversight committees must submit quarterly reports detailing variance analysis. Discrepancies under 0.01% are automatically adjusted.

Compliance Note

"Transparency is not merely a rule, but the currency of trust."

Article 1.3: Conflict of Interest

Governance

Members holding positions in external financial entities must declare holdings prior to voting on related protocol changes. Recusal is mandatory if personal gain exceeds 5% of proposed value.

Authorized by the High Council

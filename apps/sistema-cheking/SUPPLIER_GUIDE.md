# SUPPLIER SUBMISSION GUIDE
**System:** Central Checking OpusMúltipla
**Target Audience:** Media Vendors and Suppliers

---

## 1. Introduction
This guide assists media partners in correctly submitting proof-of-performance (Checking) through our centralized platform. Following these rules is **mandatory** for payment validation and processing.

---

## 2. General Instructions

### 2.1 PI Status and Blocking (CRITICAL)
When you enter your PI number, the system will check its status. If the PI has already been processed or closed, you will see a blocking message:

*   **Green ("Checking Confirmado")**: PI is approved. No further action needed.
*   **Red ("Checking Recusado")**: PI was refused (e.g., failure to air). Contact the agency representative.
*   **Amber ("Checking Arquivado")**: PI has pending issues or has been archived.
*   **Available**: If the PI is free, upload fields will appear normally.

### 2.2 Formats and Sizes
*   **Max Size:** 500MB total per submission.
*   **Supported Formats:**
    *   Images: JPG, PNG
    *   Documents: PDF, XLSX, CSV
    *   Video: MP4, MOV
    *   Audio: MP3, WAV

---

## 3. Guide by Media Type

### 3.1 Outdoor (OD, PO) & Frontlight (FL, PF)
The system will automatically list all addresses associated with the PI.
*   For **EACH** address, you must upload:
    1.  **Close-up Photo** (Focus on the creative/readability).
    2.  **Wide Photo** (Focus on context/location).
*   *Note: ZIP files are no longer required for OOH; upload directly to each slot.*

### 3.2 Digital Out of Home (DO, PH)
Requires 3 attachments + Insertion Count:
1.  **Photographic Report** (Near/Far photos).
2.  **Display Log** (Broadsign/4YouSee logs in Excel/PDF).
3.  **Proof Video** (5-10s clip of the ad running).
4.  **Extra Field:** Enter the total number of display counts (insertions).

### 3.3 TV and Radio (TV, RD)
1.  **RVC/Log:** Official broadcast log or declaration.
2.  **Extra Field (TV):** Total number of spots aired.
3.  **Spot Recording:** For live actions or testimonials, attach the MP3 recording.
